Game Target

There are four keys:up/down/left/right. Players use those buttons to move forward and avoid beetles. Every time players cross the river, there will be rewards.

Integration Rules

As time goes on and players score increases, beetles will get faster. Correspondingly, players get higher bouns points each time he crosses the river or gets props.

Two consecutive reaching river in a short internal time interval, bonus points will raise 1 time, the maximum can be added to 4 times times. If the river is interrupted continuously, it will be reduced to orginal reward

Prop Function

Rock ：Block the players' forward route

Blue Gem：Slows the beetles' speed for 5 seconds.

Orange Gem：All beetles move beyond the left side of the canvas.

Green Gem：Delete a beetle random, delete two beetles in the later of the game. If there is only one beetle left in the current canvas, it will be replaced by a medium bouns points.

Key ：Delete a rock random, and get a few points at the same time.

Heart ：Add one life，if players have five lifes, the bouns points are obtained instead.

Star ：Get a lot of bouns points
